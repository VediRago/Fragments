# Prose IV — Abby Left

His cellphone hits the street.

One hand inside the pocket, the other widening it as if it would help.

Bumping his glasses as he presses the keys against his face when he lowers his head to fit the key, a knock slides it open.

Pushing the door he runs in.

“ABBY!!!”

When a glance toward the kitchen then down the hall.

“ABBY! Cmon—we gotta move now!”

Turning right toward the stairs only to fumble on the first step, using his hands to climb while trying to regain balance, he reaches the top running his shoulder against the wall.

“Daddy!”

His hand straightens the glasses in a fast stride toward the room.

Using his elbow to open the door—

“Sera! Where is mommy?”

“Mommyyy!”

Sera waves her plushy.

As he turns his back—

“Sera, where is mommy sweety?” he stops at the door looking both ways as if he had missed something.

Turning back—

“BbBaby… where mommy?”

“Mommy!!!!”

Sera keeps waving her plushy and it hits him.

A folded piece of paper sways toward the carpet.

Sera’s voice starts to become muffled.

As he’s about to pick it up, it slowly becomes two.

Reverting back before he picks it up.

“D….y!”

His legs grow weak, one hand searching for support behind his back.

He gives out missing the bed straight toward the floor.

A shaking hand keeps it always in sight.

He drops it, one arm holding the paper between his knees and the other supporting his head.

“…y!”

A plushy lands on his feet forcing him to look up, but he doesn’t see anything.

He lifts his head still failing to reach composure.

He opens it.

Smushed letters ahead of his eyes gradually shape into words.

“Phil…”

His breathing destabilizes the letter. Still, he tries to read.

“…y!”

“I dont have to tell you how hard this is, its impossible and you know…”

His head falls back as the letter touches his chest.

His hands start to crumble it as a stream of tears guides him downward to the letter he must face.

As he turns it over, tears fall over the smudged words she wrote.

“Its been what? 23 y eAR s”

“I jus y u ay 27”

“Save her! Phill we knew this would happen.”

“We saved tHo se woR Ds for tHi\_s”

“Here there are…”

“I e u”
