# Devaar Family

## Purpose

This file defines Fedra, Valkare, and Erit's behavior throughout the story.

The Devaar family should not feel like simple villains.

They are powerful, wealthy, and capable of causing harm, but the emotional center of the family is fear: two parents trying to save their daughter and slowly making more dangerous choices because they cannot accept losing her.

---

## Erit

Erit is loved by both parents.

She grows up surrounded by privilege and wealth, but she remains kind.

Her condition gradually worsens throughout her life.

Erit should feel like a person before she becomes the reason other people make desperate decisions.

---

## Fedra

Fedra is emotionally driven.

Her fear of losing Erit influences many of her decisions.

She often reacts before thinking.

Fedra's love is real, but that love can become dangerous when fear takes over.

---

## Valkare

Valkare is more controlled and methodical.

He tries to solve problems through research, resources, and systems.

Much of his work focuses on saving Erit.

He may appear colder than Fedra, but his actions are still shaped by fear and grief.

---

## Family Dynamic

Fedra and Valkare genuinely love Erit.

Their actions are motivated by fear, grief, and desperation rather than malice.

Concern for Erit increasingly influences their decisions.

The tragedy should come from love narrowing into obsession, not from cruelty for its own sake.

---

## Kael and Sera

Fedra and Valkare eventually care about both girls.

They never intend to harm Kael.

Many later conflicts arise from misunderstanding and incomplete information.

From Kael and Sera's perspective, the Devaar family can still feel frightening, controlling, and impossible to trust. Both sides can be emotionally true at the same time.

---

## Preservation

Preservation research begins as an attempt to save Erit.

The family views preservation as a final option rather than a desired outcome.

It should feel like the last door they are willing to open because every better answer has failed.
