## Self

The Self is the blueprint from which Fragments emerge.

## Fragment

A Fragment is a different outcome explored from the same Self.

Fragments exist because the system explores infinite outcomes.

Lucreta, Sera, Kael and Erit are Fragments.

Fragments are not separate people.

## Condition

The Condition exists in the Self.

Because Fragments originate from the same Self, the Condition affects all Fragments.

The Condition causes early death.
