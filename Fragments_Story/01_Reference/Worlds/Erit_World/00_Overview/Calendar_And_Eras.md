# Calendar And Eras — Erit World

## Purpose

This file locks the broad calendar and era structure for Erit's world.

It should explain when eras begin and why they matter.

## Calendar Layers

### ACD / Celcinus Calendar

Before the Regulatus era, the world uses the older Celcinus Calendar.

This calendar belongs to the old civic order before House Ventari rewrites history through Viriatus and Regulatus.

### AVD

AVD means:

> After Viriatus's Death

Important clarification:

AVD does not begin simply because Viriatus privately dies.

AVD begins when Ermerus publicly declares Regulatus.

This means Year 0 AVD marks the moment House Ventari successfully defines what Viriatus's death means.

The private truth is murder.

The public story is noble death in war.

The calendar begins with the public interpretation, not the private event alone.

## Empiran And Regulatus Clarification

Empiran is the later word for empire.

It refers to the empire created after House Ventari replaces the old Rome-derived order with Regulatus and later dynastic systems.

Regulatus is not the empire itself.

Regulatus is the order used to control the masses inside the empire.

During Viriatus's lifetime, House Ventari uses him as an asset for control and as public proof that Ventari discipline can produce what the old order could not.

After Regulatus and AVD, that empire consolidates.

Over generations, Ventari expansion and Regulatus control allow the dynasty to control the world.

The order must stay clear:

> Viriatus conquers under Ventari control  
> -> Ecalus dies  
> -> family mourns  
> -> Ilunis murders Viriatus privately  
> -> Ermerus masks the murder  
> -> public story becomes noble death in war

Ecalus starts the plot and dies before personally creating the final new order.

After his death, Ilunis kills Viriatus privately.

Ermerus then controls the aftermath and declares Regulatus.

Regulatus is what allows the empire to consolidate as a controllable order after Viriatus's death.

## Four Eras

### 01 — Founding Era

Dramatic function:

> how the Regulatus order began

This era begins with the discovery of Viriatus and the plot started by Ecalus Ventari.

It ends with the declaration of Regulatus and the beginning of Year 0 AVD.

Core transformation:

> hidden origin -> Ventari control -> public displeasure -> Viriatus as trained asset and proof -> private murder -> Regulatus replaces the old order

### 02 — Fracture Era

Dramatic function:

> how Ermerus's controlled legacy fractured through his uncontrolled bloodline

Regulatus does not break.

The Ventari family structure fractures.

Ermerus creates the first era by controlling Viriatus's death and establishing Regulatus, but his many bastard or disputed children weaken his line.

Rutare and Ilunis descendants use legitimacy, blood purity, and protection of order to take power away from Ermerus descendants.

Core transformation:

> controlled public legacy -> uncontrolled bloodline legacy -> Rutare and Ilunis takeover

### 03 — Devaar Rise Era

Dramatic function:

> how the rejected Teresta line returned

This era happens after a major time jump.

Teresta's line, once pushed out, returns through usefulness.

Medicine, preservation, chips, augmentation, and bodily dependency become the path back into power.

House Devaar forms or rises in this era.

Core transformation:

> rejected line -> medical usefulness -> bodily dependency -> restored authority

### 04 — Erit Era

Dramatic function:

> what the world became by the time Erit, Sera, and Kael enter the story pressure

This is the modern inherited world of Erit's branch.

By this era, House Devaar has major power through medicine, preservation, chips, augmentation, and treatment access.

The old Ventari wounds survive through daily systems: identity, access, law, status, dependency, and control.

Core transformation:

> old wounds -> inherited systems -> lived pressure

## Current Rule

Do not build all eras equally yet.

The active focus is the Founding Era.

The other eras exist to hold future consequences without forcing them into the first generation.
