# House Ventari — Founding Era

## Purpose

This file explains the founding-era authority structure of House Ventari.

It should prevent confusion between:

- what House Ventari does in the Founding Era
- what later lines become after the Fracture Era
- what House Devaar becomes much later

## Core Cause

The Founding Era happens because of a plot Ecalus Ventari starts.

Ecalus takes control of Viriatus after the child is found.

House Ventari hides the truth of Viriatus's arrival and turns him into:

- weapon
- proof
- advantage
- myth
- political leverage

The original goal is not to create a moral order.

The goal is control.

House Ventari uses Viriatus to control the old order's transition into the emerging Empiran order.

After Regulatus and AVD, that emerging order consolidates into the Empiran.

Over generations, Ventari expansion turns Empiran rule into world rule.

## Relationship To Regulatus

House Ventari does not follow Regulatus the way ordinary citizens are expected to follow it.

House Ventari uses Regulatus.

Regulatus becomes the public system that teaches the masses obedience, gratitude, usefulness, hierarchy, belonging, and pride.

To citizens, Regulatus feels like order.

To House Ventari, Regulatus is a tool of control.

## Founding Authority Map

### Ecalus Ventari

Ecalus starts the plot.

He hides the truth of Viriatus's arrival and uses him as proof that House Ventari has been chosen, favored, or uniquely capable of shaping the future.

At first, Ecalus uses Viriatus as weapon, proof, and advantage.

Later, he begins seeing Viriatus as the son he wished he had.

That emotional shift wounds his actual children and creates the conditions for the later murder.

### Viriatus

Viriatus is the center of the founding plot.

He is not the creator of Regulatus.

He is the power, symbol, and public myth House Ventari builds around.

His victories make Ventari control possible.

His death makes Regulatus possible.

### Ermerus Ventari

Ermerus does not take an institution in the Founding Era.

He controls interpretation.

He controls both brothers by understanding what they need to believe.

He turns Ilunis's jealousy into family duty.

After the murder, he controls the aftermath and turns public grief into Regulatus.

His authority is meaning, not office.

### Rutare Ventari

Rutare does not found bureaucracy yet.

He survives through procedure.

Because he is overprotected and insecure, he records, orders, files, counts, and formalizes.

In the Founding Era, this makes him useful to the family structure.

Later, his line can turn procedure into administration.

### Ilunis Ventari

Ilunis does not found law yet.

He becomes force inside the family structure.

Because he is overused, maimed, humiliated, and then directed by Ermerus, he becomes the person who acts when the family needs Viriatus removed.

Later, his line can turn force into law, punishment, military authority, and sanctioned violence.

### Teresta Ventari

Teresta does not become a ruling support in the Founding Era.

She learns the truth through Rutare and distances herself from House Ventari.

Her refusal creates the beginning of a rejected line.

House Devaar does not exist yet.

Devaar belongs much later, after rejection, time, medicine, preservation, chips, augmentation, and bodily dependency.

## Founding Chain

> Ecalus starts the plot  
> -> House Ventari hides Viriatus's origin  
> -> Viriatus becomes weapon, proof, advantage, and myth  
> -> House Ventari uses him to control the old order's transition into the emerging Empiran order  
> -> Viriatus becomes beloved and powerful  
> -> Ecalus begins loving him as the son he wished he had  
> -> the Ventari children are wounded differently  
> -> Ermerus turns Ilunis's jealousy into duty  
> -> Ilunis kills Viriatus privately  
> -> Ermerus controls the aftermath  
> -> public grief becomes Regulatus  
> -> AVD begins when Regulatus is declared  
> -> the emerging Empiran order consolidates after Regulatus

## Constraint

Do not treat the Founding Era as a clean institutional chart.

This is not yet a stable system.

It is a family plot, a hidden crime, and a public interpretation that becomes world order.
