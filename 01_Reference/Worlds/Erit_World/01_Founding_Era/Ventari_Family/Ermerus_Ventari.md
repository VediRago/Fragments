# Ermerus Ventari — Founding Era

## Core Identity

Ermerus is the last son of House Ventari.

His gift is seeing what people need to believe before they know it.

He uses the same talent at two scales:

- private wound -> family duty
- public grief -> civic duty

## Core Function

Ermerus turns meaning into control.

He does not create power through strength like Ilunis or procedure like Rutare.

He creates power by interpreting events for other people.

## Key Wound / Irony

Ermerus creates controlled public legacy through Viriatus's death and Regulatus.

But his own private legacy becomes uncontrolled because of bastard or disputed children.

This becomes the root irony of the Fracture Era.

## Ilunis Manipulation Line

Ermerus manipulates Ilunis through the line:

> After all these years, Father still does not see your wound. You are maimed, barely able to walk, and still he looks at Viriatus like the son he wished he had. You threw your life away for Father, and he gave the place beside him to someone who is not even our blood.

The hinge is:

> our blood

Ermerus turns Ilunis's jealousy into duty.

Later, he turns public grief into Regulatus.

## Founding Era Role

- Recognizes Ilunis's wound.
- Gives Ilunis a reason to see killing Viriatus as family duty.
- Controls the aftermath of Viriatus's murder.
- Rewrites private murder into public noble death.
- Uses public grief to declare Regulatus.
- Begins AVD through public declaration, not private death.

## Later Consequence

Ermerus creates the first controlled legacy of the new era.

But because his own bloodline becomes disputed, later Rutare and Ilunis descendants use legitimacy and blood purity to take power away from his line.
