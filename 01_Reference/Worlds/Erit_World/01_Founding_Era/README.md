# 01 — Founding Era

## Dramatic Function

The Founding Era explains how the Regulatus order began.

This era contains Viriatus, Ecalus Ventari, the first Ventari family fracture, the murder of Viriatus, the public lie, the declaration of Regulatus, and the beginning of AVD.

## Core Question

> How did the Regulatus order begin from a hidden crime?

## Current Focus

- Viriatus is found young after a light falls from the heavens.
- Servan find him first.
- House Ventari hides the truth and shapes him.
- Ecalus Ventari first uses Viriatus as weapon, proof, and advantage.
- Later Ecalus begins seeing Viriatus as the son he wished he had.
- This wounds Rutare, Ilunis, Ermerus, and Teresta in different ways.
- Ilunis kills Viriatus privately after Ecalus dies.
- Ermerus controls the aftermath and turns public grief into Regulatus.
- AVD begins when Ermerus publicly declares Regulatus, not simply when Viriatus privately dies.
