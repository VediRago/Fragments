# 04 — Erit Era

## Dramatic Function

The Erit Era explains what the world became.

This is the era closest to the main Fragments story pressure around Erit, Sera, Kael, House Devaar, augmentation, identification, and bodily dependency.

## Core Question

> What does it feel like to live inside a world built from old wounds?

## Current Focus

- House Devaar is a restored pillar-level power.
- Medicine, preservation, augmentation, chips, and treatment access shape daily life.
- Identity is tied to bodily systems and administrative recognition.
- Erit's family lives inside inherited power and medical desperation.
- Sera and Kael entering this world creates pressure around resemblance, dependency, trust, and control.
