# Founding Lines

## Purpose

This file defines the four founding lines that grow from the wealthy family that took in and used the displaced founder.

Their names are not only family names.

They are keys.

Together, their first letters form **ERIT**:

- **E**rmerus
- **R**utare
- **I**lunis
- **T**eresta

This creates a hidden bridge between the oldest dynastic structure of the world and Erit herself.

---

## Name Bridge

**Erit** carries the sense of **will be / shall be**.

This mirrors **Será** in Portuguese, which also means **will be**.

So Sera and Erit are linked through the idea of becoming, future state, and what something will become.

This is not only a language detail.

It supports the larger structure of Fragments:

> Sera and Erit become bridges to what the world is becoming.

The four founding lines also become bridges to Erit because their initials form her name.

---

## Design Method For The Lines

Each line needs more than a political function.

Each line needs a cause.

The path should be:

> experience → emotional wound → behavior → historical path → modern system

This prevents the houses from feeling like abstract departments.

They should not become:

> This house controls law because the world needs someone to control law.

They should become:

> This person lived through something, reacted to it, and that reaction became a family pattern that hardened into history.

The names are keys, but the emotions are the roots.

---

## Core Function

The founding lines are the paths that transform the founder's stolen legacy into the current state of Erit's world.

The displaced founder is the first stone.

The family that uses him becomes the builders.

The four lines become the roads that carry his myth, usefulness, and wound forward through history.

Each line should eventually answer one question:

> Which part of the founder's legacy did this line turn into power?

## Ermerus

### Current Concept

To be developed.

### Emotional Cause

To be defined.

### Possible Historical Path

Ermerus may become the line of law, civic identity, formal order, and administrative power.

This line could shape how people are recognized, recorded, ranked, and made legible to the state.

### Key Question

What did Ermerus experience that made control through structure feel necessary?

---

## Rutare

### Current Concept

Rutare is the eldest child.

He is the most sheltered.

Because he is protected from too much of the world, he becomes shy, socially awkward, and unable to command people naturally.

This causes him to lose much of the inherited social power expected of him.

### Emotional Cause

Rutare's wound is inadequacy.

He is supposed to inherit authority, but does not feel able to hold it in the open.

Because of this, his line may eventually seek forms of power that do not require charisma: records, procedure, inheritance systems, bureaucratic legitimacy, or hidden control.

### Possible Historical Path

Rutare may become a line that turns weakness into structure.

If he cannot rule through presence, his descendants may learn to rule through systems.

### Key Question

What does Rutare build so that power no longer depends on being personally strong?

---

## Ilunis

### Current Concept

Ilunis is the second son.

He shows strength, determination, and the ability to act.

Because of that, his father uses him extensively.

He is sent into battle, shaped by expectation, and eventually maimed.

### Emotional Cause

Ilunis's wound is exploitation.

He is useful because he is strong, and that usefulness costs him his body.

This creates jealousy and anger toward the displaced founder, especially if the founder is loved, mythologized, or seen as destined for rule while Ilunis is treated as a tool.

This becomes the emotional cause for Ilunis to betray and kill the adopted son.

### Possible Historical Path

Ilunis may become the line of force, military legitimacy, sacrifice, and sanctioned violence.

His descendants may justify power through protection, battle, discipline, and the idea that some bodies must be spent to preserve the whole.

### Key Question

How does Ilunis turn being used into a doctrine of necessary sacrifice?

---

## Teresta

### Current Concept

To be developed.

### Emotional Cause

To be defined.

### Possible Historical Path

Teresta may become the line of preservation, bodies, medicine, inheritance, and augmentation.

This line could eventually lead toward Erit and the Devaar family.

### Key Question

What did Teresta inherit from the founder's wound and turn into bodily power?

---

## Design Rule

Do not fully build the lines all at once.

Each line should grow only when it explains story pressure, character behavior, social conflict, or player-facing choices.

The names are keys, not decoration.

They should eventually land on the current state of the world by explaining how old family paths became modern systems.
