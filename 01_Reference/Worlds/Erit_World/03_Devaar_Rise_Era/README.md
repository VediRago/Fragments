# 03 — Devaar Rise Era

## Dramatic Function

The Devaar Rise Era explains how the rejected line returned.

This era happens after a significant time jump.

Teresta's line, once pushed away from the original pillar structure, returns through usefulness rather than sacred origin.

## Core Question

> How does a rejected bloodline become indispensable?

## Current Focus

- Teresta's line survives outside the original ruling structure.
- A doctor or medical figure becomes the turning point.
- Medicine, preservation, augmentation, chips, and bodily dependency become the path back into power.
- The line becomes necessary because people depend on what it controls.
- House Devaar forms from this return.
- Devaar regains pillar-level importance not through founding myth, but through practical dependency.

## Constraint

This era should make House Devaar feel like an evolved historical consequence.

Do not make Devaar powerful only because the plot needs Erit's family to be powerful.

Their power should come from dependency: bodies, treatment, identification, preservation, and access.
