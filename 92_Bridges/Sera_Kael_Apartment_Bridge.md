# Sera / Kael Apartment Bridge

## Purpose

This bridge connects Sera's isolated survival phase to her first sustained bond with Kael and sets up the Devaar family's arrival.

It is not just a logistics bridge.

It establishes how Sera creates shelter before she has anyone to share it with, then turns that shelter into Kael's first fragile home.

## Core Chain

```text
Sera finds the abandoned apartment
↓
Bright sky enters through the broken apartment
↓
This marks Sera's transition while alone
↓
Sera later finds Kael in the alley
↓
Sera brings / hides Kael in the apartment
↓
A private survival routine begins
↓
Sera teaches Kael reading and writing
↓
Kael's condition worsens
↓
Sera tries to get medicine / triggers the signal
↓
The Devaars find them
↓
Kael is too weak to resist
↓
Fedra hugs Sera
```

## Important Clarification

The abandoned apartment comes before Kael.

Sera finds the space while she is still alone.

The bright sky motif enters at this stage, through the broken apartment, because it marks Sera's own transition from isolation into the possibility of shelter.

The sky is not introduced because of Kael.

It belongs to Sera first.

## Sky Motif Function

### Lucreta

Bright sky represents life outside the room, the future she will not reach.

### Sera

Bright sky enters through a broken apartment, representing life entering a ruined space before Sera knows who she will share it with.

### Hospital Girl

Bright sky becomes evidence that life continues after the condition no longer resolves.

## Emotional Function

Sera finds shelter first.

Then she finds someone worth bringing into it.

This makes the apartment more than a hiding place. It becomes Sera's first self-made threshold between loneliness and attachment.

## Relationship Function

The apartment allows Sera and Kael to form a daily-life bond before larger systems find them.

Possible beats inside the apartment phase:

- stolen blankets
- broken windows / cracked ceiling
- improvised food and water
- Sera pretending she knows what she is doing
- Kael watching words like unfamiliar objects
- Sera teaching reading and writing
- Kael writing her name while ignoring worsening symptoms
- blood falling onto the page
- Sera trying not to panic
- Kael pushing through because being understood matters more than being safe

## Devaar Arrival Function

The Devaars do not overcome Kael.

They arrive after the condition has already beaten her.

By then, Kael is too weak to resist.

Fedra's hug of Sera should feel like the first adult recognition of what Sera has been trying to do: keep another child alive.

## Historical / Structural Punch

Sera becomes home before anyone rescues them.
