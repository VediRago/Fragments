# City NPC Archetypes — Dark Time

## Purpose

This file stores reusable NPC archetypes for the Dark Time game world around 2600 AVD.

These are not named characters.

They are templates for building quest givers, witnesses, merchants, allies, enemies, and background citizens.

NPCs should be built using the project method:

> world pressure -> character need -> obstacle -> possible approaches -> consequence

---

## 1. Block Worker

### Role

Industrial laborer, maintenance worker, transport worker, refinery worker, or factory hand.

### World Pressure

Overcrowding, wage stagnation, ration shortages, dangerous labor, and no realistic upward mobility.

### Character Need

Keep themselves or their family alive.

### Obstacle

Legal work barely provides enough food, water, or rent to survive.

### Common Appearance

Worn clothes, tired eyes, grease stains, work tools, lunch tin, old injuries.

### Personality

Exhausted, practical, resigned.

### Common Lines

> Another day.

> Maybe next year.

> Work starts in twenty.

> Could be worse.

---

## 2. Gang Runner

### Role

Messenger, lookout, smuggler, courier, or errand runner for a district gang.

### World Pressure

No hope through legal paths, gang territory, poverty, and the need for belonging.

### Character Need

Money, identity, protection, and purpose.

### Obstacle

Leaving the gang may mean losing protection, income, and community.

### Common Appearance

Fast movements, nervous eyes, cheap weapon, hidden package, patched jacket.

### Personality

Quick, ambitious, frightened, loyal to the block.

### Common Lines

> Need something moved?

> Didn't see anything.

> Wrong street, friend.

> Everybody works for somebody.

---

## 3. Lux Volunteer

### Role

Aid worker, soup hall helper, shelter assistant, clinic aide, or burial helper.

### World Pressure

Endless hunger, sickness, grief, and poverty after the flare.

### Character Need

Help people without losing faith in the work.

### Obstacle

There are always more people in need than Lux can feed, shelter, or heal.

### Common Appearance

Simple clothing, Lux mark, exhausted smile, clean hands made rough by work.

### Personality

Kind, patient, tired.

### Common Lines

> Sit.

> Eat first.

> You look tired.

> The Light finds everyone eventually.

---

## 4. Scutis Patrolman

### Role

Urban order enforcer and street-level arm of state authority.

### World Pressure

Crime, riots, gang violence, black markets, and cities too large for ordinary governance.

### Character Need

Maintain order before disorder spreads.

### Obstacle

Citizens fear them, gangs test them, and every district hides more problems than procedure can solve.

### Common Appearance

Armored exosuit, riot plating, integrated communications, heavy urban gear, never fully relaxed.

### Personality

Suspicious, controlled, certain.

### Common Lines

> Identify yourself.

> Move along.

> Discipline or Grave.

> Compliance prevents casualties.

---

## 5. Corner Merchant

### Role

Small trader, food seller, cigarette seller, junk dealer, rumor broker, or block shopkeeper.

### World Pressure

Scarcity, gang taxes, ration instability, theft, and constant price changes.

### Character Need

Stay open and stay alive.

### Obstacle

Everything costs more, everyone wants credit, and every faction wants information.

### Common Appearance

Small barred shop, too many locks, old counter, hidden weapon, shelves half-empty.

### Personality

Watchful, cynical, socially sharp.

### Common Lines

> Cash first.

> Didn't hear that from me.

> Prices went up.

> Everything's expensive except bad news.

---

## 6. Old Veteran

### Role

Former soldier, retired worker, old gladiator, former Virtus auxiliary, or survivor of earlier conflicts.

### World Pressure

Memory of a better or at least different time, physical decline, and watching the city decay.

### Character Need

Leave something behind before being forgotten.

### Obstacle

Younger people are too tired to care, and institutions prefer clean official memory.

### Common Appearance

Old medals, missing teeth, prosthetic limb, worn coat, slow movements, sharp eyes.

### Personality

Bitter, observant, unexpectedly protective.

### Common Lines

> City used to breathe.

> Nobody remembers the sky.

> Everybody says they're saving the world.

> World doesn't want saving.
