# Era Four — Dark Time Notes

## Core Theme

The Dark Time was not born from collapse.

It was born from centuries of power concentrating into fewer hands.

The solar flare did not destroy civilization. It destroyed certainty.

The people had already lost hope.

The rulers lost confidence.

## Society Around 2600 AVD

Most citizens own very little:

- a bed
- ration access
- poor water
- a cramped apartment block

Social mobility is almost nonexistent.

The same bloodlines have ruled for millennia.

The same institutions survive every crisis.

Many citizens no longer believe life will improve.

## Crime And Gangs

The state owns the cities.

The gangs own the streets.

People do not join gangs because they love crime.

People join gangs because it is often the only path left toward a meaningful life.

Gangs provide:

- belonging
- protection
- purpose
- money
- identity

## Vys Atmosphere

Vys feels like a brutalist megacity.

- concrete towers
- endless apartment blocks
- fog
- steam
- refinery haze
- sirens
- distant gunfire
- exposed infrastructure
- overcrowding

The sky is visible only in fragments between towers.

The city has everything except a future.

## Scutis

Scutis patrol the streets in armored exosuits.

They are the visible face of state authority.

Their official purpose is public safety.

Their practical purpose is maintaining order inside cities too large for ordinary governance.

## Tone

The world survived.

Nobody is sure it deserved to.
