# Fragments — Design Evolution Timeline

## Purpose

A portfolio-facing timeline focused on design discoveries rather than lore chronology.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation

Goal: Create a civilization capable of surviving thousands of years.

Discovery: Regulatus evolves from government into civilization itself.

Outcome:
- Viriatus
- Regulatus
- Era structure
- Bloodlines

Skills:
- Systems thinking
- Long-term world design

---

## Phase 2 — Institutional Growth

Goal: Explore what happens when institutions succeed.

Discovery: Success concentrates power.

Outcome:
- Virtus
- Lux
- Bloodlines as institutions
- City-state integration

Skills:
- Political systems design
- Consequence mapping

---

## Phase 3 — Social Decay

Goal: Explore the cost of stability.

Discovery: People lose hope before society collapses.

Outcome:
- Dark Time
- Gang culture
- Social stagnation
- Brutalist Vys

Skills:
- Social simulation
- Environmental storytelling

---

## Phase 4 — Player Era

Goal: Find the strongest point to begin gameplay.

Discovery: The best stories happen when every faction is wounded.

Outcome:
- 2600 AVD start
- Post-flare society
- Judge Dredd atmosphere
- No clear heroes

Skills:
- Narrative framing
- Setting selection

---

## Phase 5 — Faction Simplification

Goal: Reduce complexity.

Discovery: Major factions answer the same question differently.

Outcome:
- Regulatus = Civilization
- Novacula = Correction
- North = Strength

Skills:
- Abstraction
- Communication design

---

## Phase 6 — NPC Creation Experiment

Goal: Create believable citizens.

Discovery: Characters are defined by pressure.

Method:
world pressure -> character need -> obstacle -> possible approaches -> consequence

Outcome:
- Worker archetypes
- Gang archetypes
- Lux archetypes
- Scutis archetypes

Skills:
- Human-centered design
- Emergent character creation

---

## Phase 7 — Quest Generation Experiment

Goal: Generate quests organically.

Discovery: Quests emerge from pressure chains.

Outcome:
- Empty Shelves
- Missing Worker
- Bless Each Step
- Discipline Or Grave
- Broken Lift
- The Way Remains

Skills:
- Quest design
- Systems-driven content

---

## Final Insight

Fragments evolved from a worldbuilding project into a study of how institutions, pressure, and human needs generate stories.

The project increasingly focuses on systems that produce factions, NPCs, conflicts, and quests naturally.
