# Dark Time Quest Seeds

## Purpose

This file stores early quest seeds for the Dark Time game world around 2600 AVD.

These are not finished quests.

They use the project method:

> world pressure -> character need -> obstacle -> possible approaches -> consequence

The goal is to keep quests grounded in the world instead of inventing isolated tasks.

---

## Quest Seed 01 — Empty Shelves

### World Pressure

Food shortages continue decades after the solar flare.

### Character Need

A mother needs food for her children.

### Obstacle

Ration allocations were reduced.

### Possible Approaches

- Wait for the next ration cycle.
- Buy from a gang black market.
- Ask Lux Aeterna for aid.
- Steal food from a warehouse.

### Consequence

Waiting risks sickness in the family.

Buying from the gang creates debt.

Asking Lux keeps the family alive but increases Lux influence over them.

Stealing food brings Scutis attention.

## Quest Seed 02 — Missing Worker

### World Pressure

Factories need labor more than ever during the Dark Time.

### Character Need

A supervisor needs a missing worker found.

### Obstacle

The worker joined a local gang because legal work no longer offered enough money or meaning.

### Possible Approaches

- Convince the worker to return.
- Report the worker to Scutis.
- Help the worker hide.
- Replace the worker with someone more desperate.

### Consequence

Returning the worker protects factory output but may destroy their chance at a better life.

Reporting them strengthens Scutis presence in the district.

Helping them hide strengthens gang influence.

Replacing them proves how disposable workers have become.

## Quest Seed 03 — Bless Each Step

### World Pressure

Crime rises around Lux aid halls because desperate people gather where food and medicine exist.

### Character Need

A Lux volunteer needs medicine delivered safely.

### Obstacle

The route crosses gang territory.

### Possible Approaches

- Escort the medicine personally.
- Negotiate passage with the gang.
- Bribe gang members.
- Request a Scutis escort.

### Consequence

A Scutis escort protects the delivery but increases fear and resentment in the district.

A gang deal creates future obligations.

A Lux-only route preserves trust but risks theft.

## Quest Seed 04 — Discipline Or Grave

### World Pressure

Scutis crack down after a gang shooting.

### Character Need

A young man has been arrested and his family needs help before the case disappears into the system.

### Obstacle

Evidence is unclear, witnesses are afraid, and Scutis procedure moves quickly.

### Possible Approaches

- Gather proof.
- Bribe an official.
- Ask gang witnesses to speak.
- Let the process continue.

### Consequence

Gathering proof may expose gang corruption or Scutis misconduct.

Bribery solves the case but deepens the local corruption network.

Gang testimony saves one person while empowering the gang.

Doing nothing teaches the district that survival means silence.

## Quest Seed 05 — The Broken Lift

### World Pressure

Infrastructure is failing across the brutalist blocks of Vys.

### Character Need

Residents need their apartment lift repaired.

### Obstacle

Replacement parts are unavailable through legal channels.

### Possible Approaches

- Buy black-market parts.
- Salvage old machinery.
- Ask Arumis technicians for help.
- Force local administration to respond.

### Consequence

Black-market parts empower local gangs.

Salvage risks dangerous failure later.

Arumis help brings technical reliability but may carry political strings.

Administrative pressure may work but draws attention to the block.

## Quest Seed 06 — The Way Remains

### World Pressure

Citizens increasingly distrust the administration but still believe Regulatus itself was meant to protect civilization.

### Character Need

An old veteran wants to preserve a community archive before the building is demolished.

### Obstacle

The archive sits inside a condemned block scheduled for redevelopment or clearance.

### Possible Approaches

- Appeal to Regulatus records.
- Ask Lux Aeterna to sponsor preservation.
- Hire gang protection.
- Leak documents proving corruption behind the demolition.

### Consequence

An official appeal may preserve the archive but sanitize it.

Lux support protects memory but folds it into Lux influence.

Gang protection keeps the archive local but criminalizes the effort.

Leaking documents may save the block while making the veteran a target.
