# Selected Prose

These samples show the writing itself.

The portfolio pages explain the questions behind the project. The prose shows those questions inside scenes: character connection, tension, loss, recognition, and payoff.

The recommended order starts with the most accessible scene and moves toward the more complex material.

## Recommended Samples

### 01 — Sera and Kael First Meeting

Source file: `04_Prose/07_Kael_and_Sera/inc_Sera_meets_Kael.md`

Best first prose sample. It shows Sera and Kael through movement, miscommunication, humor, and first contact.

Why it matters: before the larger ideas can work, the characters need to feel alive.

### 02 — Sera Final Scene / Basement Rupture

Source file: `04_Prose/09_Convergence/inc_Erit_body_found_basement_rupture.md`

This sample helped me understand that emotional impact does not always need more explanation. Sometimes the connection is already there.

Why it matters: a few details can carry emotional weight when the story has already built the connection.

### 03 — Unity / Hospital Ending

Source file: `04_Prose/10_Unity/Prose_Unity.md`

The most complex sample. It brings together grief, repetition, reduced description, overlapping lives, and the hospital ending.

Why it matters: this is where many project ideas meet, but the emotional focus remains on Kael.

## Reading Note

The prose does not explain the whole story by itself.

That is intentional.

The samples are selected to show character, feeling, and execution. The larger structure is explained in the portfolio notes and development process.
